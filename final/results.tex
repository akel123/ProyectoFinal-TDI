\begin{theorem*}[5.4]
    If $g(w)$ is thrice differentiable in a neighborhood of $0$ and $g^{\prime\prime} (0) > 0$, then $\eta_{w^2}(A) \leq \eta_g(A)$; in particular, $\eta_{w^2}(A) \leq \eta_{\operatorname{log}}(A)$
\end{theorem*}
